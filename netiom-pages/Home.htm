Welcome !

This site is used to demonstrate the power and abilities of the NETIOM embedded web server. NETIOM is a stand alone WEB server capable of monitoring 16 digital and 4 analogue inputs. It can also control 16 digital outputs. Everything you see here is served through NETIOM.

 The bottom half of this page shows some of the capabilities of NETIOM "Inputs"section shows the current status of of the inputs and "Commands" section issues command to toggle outputs.
